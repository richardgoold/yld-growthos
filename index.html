Working Framework

YLD GrowthOS

Growth is not one activity. It is the system that connects how we understand the market, how we win work, and how we deliver impact for clients. This is a working view of that system - built to start a conversation, not to prescribe an answer.

How to use this
Before the session

Read through each dimension and the direction of travel for each. Come prepared with a view on where we are today - what is working and what needs to change.

In the session

We will work through each dimension together as a team, agree a maturity rating, and capture what is working, what needs to change, the very next step, and who owns it.

The rating

Each dimension gets one of three maturity ratings agreed as a team. The goal is an honest, shared picture - not all greens.

The output

For each dimension we capture four things: what is working, what needs to change, the very next step, and who owns it. The facilitator also captures any wider notes from the discussion.
